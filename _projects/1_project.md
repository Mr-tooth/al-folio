**Project Period:** 2025.04 – 2026.01
**Funding:** MoE Integrated Platform Project (教育部集成攻关大平台项目)

## Overview

Research on heterogeneous reconfiguration technology for legged robots, enabling multi-modal locomotion capabilities including complex terrain walking, fall recovery, quadruped crawling, and vehicle riding/dismounting.

## Key Contributions

- Designed **extended command space** and **VAE-based state estimation** for implicit multi-modal motion control
- Developed reinforcement learning training pipeline with **sim-to-real deployment**
- Achieved complex terrain walking, fall recovery, quadruped crawling, and vehicle riding/dismounting

## Technical Highlights

- Variational Autoencoder (VAE) for latent state representation
- Multi-modal motion control via reinforcement learning
- Sim-to-real transfer on custom legged robot platform
