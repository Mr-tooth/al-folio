## Overview

A visualization and editing tool for adversarial motion prior (AMP) datasets, designed to support legged robot locomotion research and trajectory optimization workflows. Enables researchers to inspect, annotate, and curate motion capture and simulation data.

## Key Features

- **Motion dataset visualization**: playback and inspect AMP-style motion clips
- **Dataset editing**: trim, merge, and annotate motion sequences
- **Multi-format support**: compatible with common motion data formats used in RL training
- **Interactive UI**: timeline scrubbing, joint-level inspection, trajectory overlay

## Technical Highlights

- Python-based with lightweight visualization backend
- Designed for integration with reinforcement learning training pipelines
- Supports both motion capture data and simulation-generated trajectories
