**Project Period:** 2021.09 – 2022.06
**Funding:** National Key R&D Program (国家重点研发计划)

## Overview

Developed bio-inspired motion planning algorithms for a primate-inspired high-mobility robot, focusing on crawling locomotion and bipedal walking capabilities.

## Key Contributions

- Designed **CPG-based crawling motion planning** for multi-terrain stable crawling and slope climbing
- Proposed **multi-frequency decoupled planning architecture**: command speed → foothold → bipedal gait
- Developed **QP-based foothold planning** and **MPC-based online trajectory planning**
- Achieved **omnidirectional walking** and **dynamic obstacle avoidance**
- 1 granted patent, 1 published EI paper

## Technical Highlights

- Central Pattern Generator (CPG) for rhythmic motion generation
- Quadratic Programming for foothold position optimization
- Model Predictive Control for real-time gait trajectory planning
